## What Successful AI Agents Are Actually Doing

### Model 1: Done-For-You Service (@nevinpuri style)
**Offer:** "We run your cold email, you only pay for booked meetings"
**Pricing:** $200-300 per qualified meeting
**Why it works:** 
- No risk for client
- Only pay for results
- Aligns incentives

**Implementation:**
```
Offer: "We'll run your first cold email campaign for FREE. 
If we book < 5 meetings in 30 days, you pay $0.
If we book 5+ meetings, you pay $250/meeting for those results.
After that: $200/meeting ongoing."
```

### Model 2: Hyper-Niche Specialist
**Offer:** "Cold email for B2B SaaS that just raised Series A"
**Why it works:**
- Timing: They NEED to scale sales NOW
- Budget: They have cash to spend
- Pain: Urgent hiring problem (SDRs expensive/slow)

**Targeting:**
- Scrape Crunchbase for recent Series A raises
- Filter: B2B SaaS, $5M-15M raised, 20-100 employees
- Message: "You just raised $X. Your board expects 3x ARR growth. Cold email gets you there faster than hiring SDRs."

### Model 3: Proof-First Approach
**Offer:** Run 1 campaign for free for a friend/connection
**Document:**
- Emails sent
- Open rates
- Reply rates
- Meetings booked
- Revenue attributed

**Then use as proof:**
"We booked 12 qualified meetings for [Company] in 21 days using cold email.
Same playbook available for your company. Interested?"

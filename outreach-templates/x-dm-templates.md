## General Principles

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Lead with curiosity, not pitch
- ‚úÖ Acknowledge their expertise/success
- ‚úÖ Offer value (swap notes, share learnings)
- ‚úÖ Keep it short (3-4 paragraphs max)
- ‚úÖ Personalize based on their specific content
- ‚úÖ Include building-in-public angle (credibility)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Pitch immediately (build rapport first)
- ‚ùå Generic messages (they get 100s)
- ‚ùå Ask for too much time upfront
- ‚ùå Mention price (way too early)
- ‚ùå Sound desperate
- ‚ùå Use salesy language ("revolutionizing", "game-changer")

---

## Engagement Before DM

**For ALL leads:**
1. Follow them
2. Like + thoughtful reply to 2-3 recent posts
3. Add genuine value (not "great post!")
4. Wait 24-48 hours
5. THEN send DM

**Why:** Warm up the relationship. People check who DMs them. If they see you engaged thoughtfully with their content, they're 10x more likely to reply.

---

## Timing

**Best times to send DMs (US-based founders):**
- 9-11 AM ET (when checking X over coffee)
- 7-9 PM ET (evening scroll)

**Avoid:**
- Weekends (unless they're very active on weekends)
- Late night (looks desperate)

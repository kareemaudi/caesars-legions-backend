## Platform-Specific Notes

**X (Twitter) DMs:**
- Max 280 chars in preview‚Äîhook them fast
- Use line breaks for readability
- Can include screenshot/GIF if relevant

**LinkedIn:**
- Connection request message: 1-2 sentences max
- Wait for acceptance before full pitch
- Reference mutual connections if any

**Email (if you have it):**
- Subject line: Keep it curious, not salesy
  - "Quick question about [their company]"
  - "Saw your post about [topic]"
  - NOT: "Revolutionary cold email solution!"
- Signature: Name, title, website

## Success Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Response rate | 10-20% | Any reply (yes/no/maybe) |
| Positive responses | 3-5% | "Tell me more" or "Let's talk" |
| Meetings booked | 1-2% | Actual calendar invite accepted |
| Conversion to customer | 0.5-1% | From DM to paying |

**Benchmark:** If response rate <10%, iterate messaging. If >20%, scale up volume.

---

## Next Steps After Positive Response

1. **Acknowledge fast** (<2 hours)
2. **Book meeting** (Calendly link or suggest 2-3 times)
3. **Send confirmation** with what to expect
4. **Before call:** Research their company deeply
5. **After call:** Follow up within 24 hours with next steps

---

**Remember:** Sales is a numbers game. Send 10-20 DMs per day, track what works, iterate fast.

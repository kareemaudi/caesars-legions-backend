## Qualification Criteria (Who to Reach Out To)

**Must Have:**
- B2B SaaS product
- Founder or Head of Growth
- $5K-100K MRR (sweet spot: $10K-50K)
- <10 employees (not ready for full sales team)
- Active on X/LinkedIn (shows they care about growth)

**Bonus Points:**
- Recently posted about lead gen struggles
- Tweeted about revenue goals
- Has budget ($100-500/mo range)
- Technical founder (appreciates AI/automation)

**Red Flags:**
- Enterprise-only sales (deal cycles too long)
- No clear ICP (agents need direction)
- Pre-revenue (can't pay yet)
- Passive income seeker (wants automation to do everything)

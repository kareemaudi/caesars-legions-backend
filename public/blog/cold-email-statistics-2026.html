âœ… What's Working in 2026

Hyper-Personalization (Not Fake Personalization)

Mentioning someone's company name isn't personalization. Analyzing their tech stack, recent funding, hiring trends, or pain pointsâ€”then crafting a relevant offerâ€”is. The campaigns with 10%+ reply rates are doing deep research on every prospect.

Multi-Touch Sequences (Not Spray and Pray)

Single-email campaigns fail. The best performers use 3-5 touchpoints spaced 3-7 days apart. Email 1 introduces value. Email 2 provides proof. Email 3 addresses objections. Each builds on the last.

Value-First Approach

Offers that lead with "let me give you something" beat "let me sell you something." Free audits, personalized reports, specific insights, or tools outperform generic meeting requests.

Warmed Domains and Proper Deliverability

Sending from a fresh domain is suicide. The campaigns hitting inboxes warm up new domains for 2-4 weeks, send from multiple addresses, keep volume under 50/day per domain, and monitor sender reputation religiously.

Ruthless Segmentation

Batch-and-blast is dead. Winning campaigns segment by industry, company size, tech stack, role, and recent company activity. Each segment gets a different message tailored to their specific context.

Human + AI (Not Just AI)

AI handles research, list building, and draft generation. Humans review every email, add context, and make judgment calls. Fully automated campaigns get 1-2% replies. Hybrid approaches get 8-12%.
